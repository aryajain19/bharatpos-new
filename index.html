From a busy kirana counter to a multi-branch textile shop — BharatPOS fits without configuration.

Fast barcode billing
Scan any EAN-13, Code128, or QR code to add items instantly. Bill a full cart in under 30 seconds with real-time stock deduction.
GSTR-1 & GSTR-3B ready
Every sale is tagged by GSTIN, rate slab, and HSN. Export portal-ready JSON in one tap — no manual entry.
Tally-style accounting
Day book, ledgers, journal entries, P&L, and balance sheet — built automatically from your daily sales data.
Live inventory
Stock deducts on every bill. Low-stock alerts and reorder tracking stop you running dry on bestsellers.
UPI & QR payments
Collect via any UPI app. Auto-generate a payment QR for each bill — no extra hardware or payment gateway setup.
Works offline too
Keep billing during internet outages. All data syncs to Firebase automatically the moment you reconnect.

Automated GSTR-1 & GSTR-3B
